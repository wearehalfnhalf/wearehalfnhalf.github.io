From creation to market

We are Micah Perrello and Giancarlo Bruno, from New Jersey, based out of New York City. Half&Half is the brand we launched in 2023 for what we make and have made in documentary, music video and narrative filmmaking.

Half&Half is an expression of our bicultural identities. We both have Italian-American fathers, and mothers from Taiwan (Micah) and Peru (Giancarlo).
